Super60 is a special batch of 30 to 60 students, chosen every year from the first year students of CSE Branch, in order to make them ready to face challenges beyond their prescribed curriculum. Students are provided with enriched learning modules based on their potential.

Extra curricular activities including reasoning, Quantitative skills, Communication Skills, Personality development are provided to students.


What We Learn



At our Department, we believe in providing comprehensive learning experiences that go beyond the confines of traditional education. Our "Super 60" program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies necessary to thrive in today's dynamic world.

Showcase Your Tailent

The presentation round is a pivotal component of our event, designed to highlight the innovation, expertise, and creativity of our Students. During this round, each team or individual will have the opportunity to showcase their project, idea, or research to our esteemed panel of judges and audience members.

Students are encouraged to craft engaging presentations that effectively communicate their objectives, methodology, findings, and potential impact. Whether you're presenting a business proposal, a scientific breakthrough, a social initiative, or a technological innovation, this is your chance to shine and captivate the audience with your ideas.
